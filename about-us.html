About us

Trair is an airline that was created many years ago at a time when it was difficult to create any company. We have been providing flight services to our users for more than 40 years. Thanks to our leadership teams, employees, flight attendants, pilots... we are one of the best airlines in the world. We own over 35 airports, where only planes from our airline take off and land. We currently have 2387 employees at the airports themselves, 328 pilots, 788 flight attendants and 246 control agents for each flight.

Each of our pilots has over 10 years of flying experience and is trained and has completed the most renowned courses out there. The flight attendants are very friendly and want to help you in any way possible, they will do everything to make you comfortable on the flight. We have one of the best business classes out there. In business class, there are separate rooms with 4 beds, 3 TVs, 2 bathrooms, 3 laptops and incredibly fast internet. There are also massage rooms, as well as a sauna, in case of longer flights, so that you can relax. Some of the aircraft we own are Boeing 747, Boeing 737, Airbus A350 XWB, Comac C919, Irkut MC-21... and many others. Each of our aircraft is equipped with small tablets in each seat, so you can relax and watch a movie during the flight. We also have a kitchen, where dishes are made according to the passengers' choice. We have an unlimited number of drinks, which the flight attendant brings to you together with the ordered food.
